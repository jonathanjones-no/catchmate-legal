# How to Delete Your CatchMate Account

You can permanently delete your CatchMate account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

## Steps

1. Open CatchMate and tap the **Profile** tab
2. Tap **Account**
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm by tapping **Delete** in the first prompt
5. Confirm again by tapping **Delete My Account** in the final prompt

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- **Profile information** (name, bio, photo, date of birth, location)
- **Matches and messages** (all conversations and match history)
- **Swipes** (all like and pass history)
- **Sessions** (all proposed, confirmed, and cancelled sessions)
- **Availability** (weekly schedule and date overrides)
- **Blocks and reports** (any users you blocked or reported)
- **Favorites** (any saved matches)
- **Push notification tokens** (device registrations)
- **Profile photos** (all uploaded images)
- **Your authentication account** (login credentials)

If you signed in with Apple, your Apple ID credential for CatchMate is also revoked.

## Data Retention

After deletion, no personal data is retained. Automated cleanup also removes:

- Left-swipe records older than 90 days
- Unmatched session proposals older than 30 days

## Data Export

Before deleting your account, you can download a copy of your data:

1. Go to **Profile** > **Account**
2. Tap **Download My Data**
3. A JSON file containing your profile, matches, messages, sessions, and other data will be shared to your device
